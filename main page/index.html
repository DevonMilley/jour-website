About Me

Devon Milley is a sophomore journalism and information science major at the University of Maryland. She has reported, copy edited and photographed for various on-campus publications including Her Campus at Maryland, The Diamondback and Stories Beneath the Shell. Devon is most interested in stories about social change and travel. In the future, she hopes to use her coding knowledge to work on data-driven stories, whether that be through data analysis or data visualization. On top of her journalistic interests, she also enjoys graphic design and coding.
