About Me

Devon Milley is a junior journalism and information science major at the University of Maryland. She is currently the assistant managing editor and deputy engagement editor at The Diamondback, the university's independent student newspaper. She is also editor-in-chief of Her Campus Maryland, an online women's publication. In the future, Devon hopes to use her dual degree to work on data-driven stories, whether that be through data analysis or data visualization. On top of her journalistic interests, she also enjoys graphic design and coding.
